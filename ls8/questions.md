
bitwise operators
    Boolean Bitwise
OR    or      |
AND   AND     &
XOR   N/A     ^
NOT   not     ~

& helps get rid of bits you don't want or isolate bits you do want

>> shifts bits right by one
<< shifts bits left by one

setting a bit to 1 using or:
    0001100
|   0000111
------------
    0001111
